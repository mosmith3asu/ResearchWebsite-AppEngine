Advancing AI through interactive experiments

Welcome! This website is intended to allow you to easily participate in experiments supporting research into cognition-aware AI partners. Our goal of enabling "cognition-aware" AI simple means providing robots or AI partners with a deeper understanding about how humans evaluate, reason, and make decisions when interacting with artificial agents. This is done by programming the artificial agent to consider well studied principles from the fields of cognitive psychology, economics, and neuroscience.

However, we need your help to validate and test these new models we are studying! If you are interested in contributing to research on cognitive-aware robotics, then please click the button below to join one of our short, interactive experiments.

Before we begin, please ensure

  • Playing on a desktop or laptop computer (not a mobile device)
  • Your internet browser window is maximized

If you have any questions please contact mosmith3@asu.edu
